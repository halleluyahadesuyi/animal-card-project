Peacock

Peacocks are huge colorful birds famous for their beautiful feathers in their tail. These long tails comprise almost 60 percent of their body length. The tail feathers have unique markings on them of blue, gold, red, dark brown, or black color.

  • Scientific Name: Pavo cristatus
  • Average Length: 2.3 metres
  • Average Lifespan:20 years
  • Habitat: Open Lowland Forests
